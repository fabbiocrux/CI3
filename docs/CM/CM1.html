1.1.3 Learn goals of Design and Innovation

  1. Give you the basic skills to be able to materialize a conceptual idea into an intermediate mechatronic design object for a given concept.

  2. Learn technologies available at the Lorraine Fab Living Lab.

  3. A scientific and technical culture needed to independently design and build a prototype of a functional mechanical system including a control section.

1.1.4 Why is it important for you as futur Engineers?

  1. Idea → Mock-up → Proof-of-Concept → Prototype

  2. To be able to choose the most appropriate design tools.
